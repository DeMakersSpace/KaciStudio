CASE 001 · Resurrack × LittleMissMarket

A co-branded drop, told as a shared ritual.

Package chosen

Event Branding & Marketing

Resurrack and LittleMissMarket needed a collab story that could hold two lifestyle audiences without feeling like a forced partnership. We shaped the launch around a shared weekend ritual: easy to understand, social-first, and made for the girls who already live in that world.

Find them on @resurrack · @littlemissmrkt · View reel
The Brief

Two distinct communities, both leaning into the same slow, intentional living aesthetic. The ask was to make the collab feel native to both brands instead of looking like a standard campaign announcement.

Our Approach

Rather than announcing the drop all at once, we let it unfold through mood, routine, and community cues. The teaser leaned into Little Miss Market's voice while the wider content system gave Resurrack and LMM a common language across TikTok and Instagram.

The Result

The launch gained a clearer pre-event hook: cute brands, cool girls, and a market made for the girls. Instead of treating the reel as a numeric proof point, the case now shows the real value: a branded invitation people could immediately understand and share.

Package chosen

Social Media Management - Atelier

Find them on @flycotravel
The Brief

CASE 006 · Happy Rei

Letting tiny reviewers make the case for comfort.

Package chosen

Social Media Management - Bespoke

Happy Rei, a kids' lifestyle brand, needed content that could show softness, usefulness, and parent trust without sounding like a catalogue. We built the reel around the brand's most honest proof point: children choosing and rating their own favourites.

Find them on @happyrei.sg · View reel
The Brief

Happy Rei sells pieces parents choose with care, but the strongest proof was already happening at home: children reaching for the same favourites again and again. The ask was to turn that everyday affection into content that felt real, warm, and easy to trust.

Our Approach

We framed the reel as a tiny review session instead of a product showcase. The children led the rhythm, the products stayed tactile and close, and the caption invited other parents into the conversation: which favourite would your little one choose?

The Result

A product story that felt like parent-to-parent proof. The reel made Happy Rei's comfort visible through genuine reactions, giving the brand a softer and more believable way to show why the pieces matter beyond how they look.

CASE 007 · Luminance Learning

Turning Math anxiety into a guided conversation.

Package chosen

Social Media Management - Bespoke

Luminance Learning, a Math tuition brand, needed to speak to students and parents without making Math feel heavier than it already can. We shaped a 60-second Q&A around the questions families quietly carry: confidence, fear, foundations, and the small wins that rebuild belief.

Find them on @luminance_learning · View reel
The Brief

Math tuition is often sold through grades, but Luminance Learning's real difference is emotional: students are seen, supported, and guided step by step. The content needed to make that care visible before a parent ever sends an enquiry.

Our Approach

We used a direct Q&A format so Mrs Khet could answer the real concerns behind Math learning: fear, weak foundations, and the need for confidence. The pacing stayed clear and human, with social copy that positioned coaching as support, not pressure.

The Result

A reel that gives parents a fast read on the teaching philosophy: calm, structured, and student-first. Instead of promising quick fixes, the content shows the trust-building work behind helping students believe in themselves again.
