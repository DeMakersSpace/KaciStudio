CASE 001 · Resurrack × LittleMissMarket

A co-branded drop, told as a shared ritual.

Strategy Shoot day TikTok Instagram Community

Resurrack and LittleMissMarket came to us ahead of a co-branded drop — two lifestyle audiences, one story to tell. We built the narrative arc across eight TikToks and a co-hosted IG shoot day, framing the collab as a "weekend in, weekend out" ritual.

Results updating — check back soon

Find them on @resurrack · @littlemissmrkt
The Brief

Two distinct communities, both leaning into the same slow, intentional living aesthetic. The ask: make the collab feel organic, not promotional.

Our Approach

Rather than announcing the drop, we let it unfold — framing it as a "weekend in, weekend out" ritual. Content lived in the everyday: Sunday markets, slow mornings. Captions led with feeling, not SKUs.

The Result

Content that felt brand-native, not agency-made. Audience overlap between both brands amplified reach without paid spend.
