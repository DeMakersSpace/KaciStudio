CASE 001 · Resurrack × LittleMissMarket

collab that stuck.

Resurrack and LittleMissMarket came to us ahead of a co-branded drop — two lifestyle audiences, one story to tell. We built the narrative arc across eight TikToks and a co-hosted IG shoot day, framing the collab as a "weekend in, weekend out" ritual rather than a product push.

Results updating — check back soon

The Brief

Resurrack and LittleMissMarket came to us ahead of a co-branded drop — two lifestyle audiences, one story to tell. Two distinct communities, both leaning into the same slow, intentional living aesthetic. The ask: make the collab feel organic, not promotional.

Our Approach

We built the narrative arc across eight TikToks and a co-hosted IG shoot day. Rather than announcing the drop, we let it unfold — framing it as a "weekend in, weekend out" ritual. Content lived in the everyday: Sunday markets, slow mornings, the kind of lifestyle both brands already represent. Captions led with feeling, not SKUs.

The Result

Content that felt brand-native, not agency-made. Audience overlap between both brands amplified reach without paid spend. Full results in progress — check back soon.

The Brief

Brixx Derma is a derm-led skincare line with rigorous formulas and a credibility problem on social. Their existing content leaned too clinical — it educated without connecting. They needed a voice that felt like a trusted friend, not a white paper.

We rebuilt their social around the concept of "bedtime, not breakthrough" — nightly routine TikToks, ingredient storytelling that felt conversational, and Telegram community drops for their most engaged followers.
