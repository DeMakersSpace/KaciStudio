Social Media Management - Atelier

The Brief

Their content had useful information, but it leaned too clinical for the way people actually talk about skin. The brief was to preserve trust while making the brand easier to read, save, and recommend.

Our Approach

We rebuilt the voice around routine, reassurance, and clear ingredient storytelling. Night routine TikToks, product-drop copy, and Telegram community updates worked together so education no longer felt separate from desire.

The Result

30K+ Instagram views with 73% from new audiences, plus +500% TikTok view growth and +1,700% TikTok likes growth. The brand became easier to follow because the content finally sounded like care, not a lecture.
