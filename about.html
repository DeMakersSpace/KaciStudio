◎ THE AGENCY · 02

Co-Founders

Meet the team behind KACISTUDIO

Jovinna
Angeline
Co-Founders · KACISTUDIO

KACISTUDIO started from a genuine passion for creating content and helping brands tell their stories better.

Before having this space, we were both creating for different brands in our own ways and understanding what makes a brand feel present online. The more we worked in this space, the more we realised how much we loved the process behind it: the ideas, the visuals, the storytelling, and the little details that help a brand feel more like itself.

Over time, we saw how our strengths naturally came together. Angeline brings the creative instinct, visual direction, and ideas, while Jovinna brings the structure, planning, and attention to detail needed to bring those ideas to life. What started as something we simply enjoyed slowly became something we wanted to build with more intention.

Our heart behind KACI has always been simple, to support brands that want to show up better, but may not always know where to begin. We know how overwhelming content can feel when you are running a business, and we wanted to create a studio that could make the process feel clearer, more thoughtful, and more aligned.

Today, KACISTUDIO is our way of bringing that passion to life, helping brands create with purpose, show up with consistency, and tell their stories in a way that feels true to them.
