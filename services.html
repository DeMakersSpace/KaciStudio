◎ Client Onboarding · 02

How we work,
step by step.

We take the time to understand your brand before we create, shaping each step with clarity, care, and intention.

In session
1 · Discovery & Alignment

We begin with a conversation to understand your brand, goals, audience, current challenges, and the kind of presence you want to build. This helps us see where your brand is now, and where we can take it with intention.

In session
2 · Brand & Social Audit

Before we create, we take time to look through your existing platforms, visuals, messaging, competitors, and overall digital presence. This allows us to identify what is working, what can be refined, and where there is room to grow.

In session
3 · Strategy & Creative Direction

From there, we shape the direction forward. This includes your content pillars, campaign angles, visual direction, tone of voice, and monthly content approach, so every idea has a purpose and every piece of content feels connected.

In session
4 · Content Planning & Creation

Once the direction is aligned, we move into planning and production. From content calendars and shoot direction to editing, design, copywriting, and asset creation, this is where the strategy begins to take form.

In session
5 · Review, Publishing & Growth

Before anything goes live, we review and refine the work together. Once approved, we schedule, publish, engage, and review the month's performance, using each cycle to understand what resonated and how we can keep building with more clarity and consistency.
