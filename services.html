◎ Client Onboarding · 02

How we work,
step by step.

Every brand walks the same five-step path. It's how we get close enough to make work that actually sounds like you.

In session
1 · Discover & align

Discover & align.

An in-person meeting to understand your brand, your voice, and your goals. We see if we're the right fit and align on values before anything else. The best work comes from being like-minded and moving with intention.

In session
2 · Research & strategy

Research & strategy.

A thorough audit of your current social presence: what's working, what's not, and where the gaps are. We also study your competitors, industry trends, and audience behaviours. From there, we build a strategy that's uniquely yours, and present it in a second meeting where we listen for your thoughts.

In session
3 · Content planning

Content planning.

With the strategy in place, we craft your content calendar: post outlines, creative direction, captions, hashtags, and storytelling angles. Every detail is curated to feel on-brand and right for each platform, while staying true to your voice.

In session
4 · Creation & approval

Creation & approval.

Then we make it. Shooting, editing, and design — all handled here. We share the final assets with you for approval, and refine before scheduling if needed.

In session
5 · Execute & elevate

Execute & elevate.

Once approved, we handle the posting and scheduling across platforms. We monitor performance, adjust based on insights, and refine each cycle so the work keeps getting better as we grow with you.
