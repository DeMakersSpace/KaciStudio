CASE 006 · Happy Rei

Letting tiny reviewers make the case for comfort.

Package chosen

Social Media Management - Bespoke

Happy Rei, a kids' lifestyle brand, needed content that could show softness, usefulness, and parent trust without sounding like a catalogue. We built the reel around the brand's most honest proof point: children choosing and rating their own favourites.

Find them on @happyrei.sg · View reel
The Brief

Happy Rei sells pieces parents choose with care, but the strongest proof was already happening at home: children reaching for the same favourites again and again. The ask was to turn that everyday affection into content that felt real, warm, and easy to trust.

Our Approach

We framed the reel as a tiny review session instead of a product showcase. The children led the rhythm, the products stayed tactile and close, and the caption invited other parents into the conversation: which favourite would your little one choose?

The Result

A product story that felt like parent-to-parent proof. The reel made Happy Rei's comfort visible through genuine reactions, giving the brand a softer and more believable way to show why the pieces matter beyond how they look.
