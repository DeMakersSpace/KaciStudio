◎ FINE PRINT · 03

Questions we
hear a lot.

(01)
What's the $500 deposit for?
It's a small commitment upfront that holds your slot and respects the time, energy, and intention we're about to pour into your brand. After month one, you decide how much you'd like to pay based on your experience and budget. We start with trust and build from there.
(02)
Can I pick and choose deliverables?
We offer full-scope management only. It's not a flex — it's the only way we can deliver the kind of cohesive, story-first social presence we're known for. One voice, one rhythm, one studio.
(03)
Do you work with brands outside Singapore?
Yes — we're Singapore-based, but our clients are global. Shoot days happen in SG; everything else (strategy, editing, management, community) works asynchronously across time zones.
(04)
How long is the commitment?
Month-to-month. After the starter deposit, every cycle ends with a review — you decide if it feels right to continue. We'd rather earn the next month than lock one in.
(05)
Who owns the content we make together?
You do. All raw footage, edits, photography, and brand assets from shoot day are yours to keep, use, and repurpose — forever.
(06)
How soon can we start?
We take on a limited number of brands per quarter so we can show up with intention. Reach out — if we're a fit, we'll propose the nearest available slot.
