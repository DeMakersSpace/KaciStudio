Melissa Shoes

CASE 004 · Melissa Shoes · by way of NYP SBM TEP

Repositioning a heritage footwear label for Gen Z.

Package chosen

Content Creation - À La Carte

Melissa Shoes already had heritage, recognisable silhouettes, and a strong fashion memory. The opportunity was to make the brand feel current again through motion-first content built for younger audiences.

Find them on @melissashoessg
The Brief

The brand did not need to explain its history. It needed content that translated that history into today: faster, more styled, more social, and more aligned with how Gen Z and millennials discover fashion.

Our Approach

We moved away from static product framing and built reels around styling, movement, and cultural context. The product stayed present, but the hook came from the feeling of wearing it.

The Result

10K+ views across campaigns and a 900%+ audience interaction uplift. The content gave a familiar brand a more current rhythm without losing its fashion identity.
