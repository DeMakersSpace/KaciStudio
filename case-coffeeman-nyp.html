Coffeeman@NYP, a campus F&B brand, needed a launch story for a new menu inside a campus environment. The content had to feel appetising and polished without making the cafe feel out of reach for the students walking past it every day.

The Brief

A new menu needed attention, but the tone mattered. The cafe had to look intentional and worth visiting while still feeling familiar to a student audience.

Our Approach

We used close-up sequences, craft-in-motion shots, and clean food pacing to make the menu feel tangible. The copy stayed warm and direct, keeping the focus on good food made well.

The Result

1K+ views per video, 92% new audiences reached, and hundreds of interactions. The launch content did its job by turning a menu update into a reason to stop by.
